Resorts World Genting Cruises

is a luxury cruise experience that departs from Port Klang, Malaysia and offers a variety of itineraries to destinations such as Thailand, Vietnam, Cambodia, and Indonesia. The cruise ship, called the Genting Dream, is a state-of-the-art vessel that can accommodate up to 3,400 passengers. The following is a brief schedule of a typical 5-night itinerary for Resorts World Genting Cruises

Schedule

  • Day 1
    : Departure from Port Klang Board the Genting Dream in the afternoon and settle into your stateroom. The ship will depart from Port Klang in the evening.
  • Day 2 : At Sea Enjoy a full day at sea to explore the ship's amenities, which include swimming pools, water slides, a rock-climbing wall, a casino, a theater, and a variety of dining and entertainment options. Day 3: Phuket, Thailand Arrive in Phuket, Thailand in the morning and spend the day exploring the island's beaches, markets, and cultural attractions.
  • Day 4 : Langkawi, Malaysia Arrive in Langkawi, Malaysia in the morning and spend the day enjoying the island's natural beauty, which includes rainforests, waterfalls, and beaches.
  • Day 5 : At Sea Spend another full day at sea to take advantage of the ship's amenities and activities. Day 6: Return to Port Klang Arrive back in Port Klang in the morning and disembark from the ship. Note that this itinerary is just an example and there are many other itineraries available with different ports of call and durations
